about us

Your Gateway to Campus Excitement and Engagement

Welcome to Anweshan 2k24 organized by MAKAUT, where students come together to celebrate learning, creativity, and community! Our mission is to provide engaging, impactful events that enhance the college experience and foster a sense of belonging among students. From technical fests to social gatherings and cultural celebrations, we organize a wide variety of events designed to inspire, educate, and entertain. At Anweshan, we believe that college life is not just about lectures and exams; it’s about building connections, discovering new passions, and creating memories that last a lifetime. We strive to create a vibrant and inclusive environment where every student feels empowered to participate, explore, and grow.

Join us for exciting events that bring the campus together and make your college experience unforgettable!
